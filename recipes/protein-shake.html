Protein Shake

Description

In this recipe, you will learn how to make a protein shake. This is a very simple recipe that anyone can learn! Once you've finished, you'll have yourself a tasty and healthy drink.

Ingredients

  • 200ml of milk
  • 1 scoop of whey protein powder
  • 1/2 cup oats
  • 1 tablespoon peanut butter
  • 1/2 banana

Steps

  1. Pour milk into blender
  2. Add whey protein powder to blender
  3. Stir ingredients inside the blender together with a spoon
  4. Add oats to belnder
  5. Repeat step 3
  6. Add peanut butter to blender
  7. Using a cuting board and knife, cut a banana in half
  8. Cut the half banana into slices
  9. Add banana slices to blender
  10. Blend ingredients for 1-2 minutes
  11. Pulse blender a few times once finished
  12. Pour ingredients from blender into a glass
  13. Enjoy!
