Scrambled Eggs

Description

In this recipe, you will learn how to make my classic scrambled eggs. I'm somebody who used to hate scrambled eggs. If they weren't on a sandwich, I typically wasn't a fan.

These eggs, however, are different than the ones I was used to. These eggs are fluffy, tasty, a great source of protein, and making them has become a staple part of my morning routine. Are you ready? Let's begin!

Ingredients

  • 2 (or more) large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons whole milk (1 tablespoon per large egg)
  • 3 dashes salt
  • 2 dashes pepper
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese(mozzarella or mexican style four cheese blend)
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl
  2. Add milk to the bowl
  3. Add salt and pepper to the bowl
  4. Add shredded cheese to the bowl
  5. Add butter to frying pan
  6. Set burner to high
  7. While butter is melting, whisk the ingredients in the bowl
  8. Once butter is melted, pour whisked ingredients of the bowl into frying pan
  9. Using a nonstick spatula, stir ingredients in frying pan
  10. Set burner to low when curds begin to form
  11. Remove frying pan from stovetop once the eggs become light and fluffy
  12. Enjoy!
